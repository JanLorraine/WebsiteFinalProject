About


"Tia Maria's Sining at Kultura is a restaurant conceptualized in an old rich culture" says by the proprietorship, Chef Maryrose Faraon - Barte, an amazing cook who has both love for food and art.


Filipino and European cuisine are served here in the restaurant. Events are also hosted here; initially incidental, they are now regularly held here on special gatherings.


Your mouth and eyes are fed by Tia Maria's amazing cuisine and gorgeous artwork. Filipino artisans created the artwork that surrounds the restaurant, which adds warmth and may possibly be for sale.

The restaurant is located at San Pablo City Laguna, Chef Maryrose's beloved hometown. When asked about the reason of establishing a restaurant, Chef replied, "I quess it was influenced by my father 2nd Lt. Reynando Faraon, who loves iconic dishes from San Pablo."
